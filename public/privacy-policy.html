Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2025

Overview

This privacy policy describes how Arthur Teller ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with our internal QuickBooks Online integration application ("the Application").

Scope

This Application is for internal business use only and is not available to the general public. It is used solely to manage bookkeeping and accounting data for Arthur Teller LLC.

Information We Access

The Application accesses QuickBooks Online data including:

  • Financial transactions (invoices, bills, payments)
  • Customer and vendor information
  • Account balances and reports
  • Journal entries

How We Use Information

All data accessed through this Application is used exclusively for:

  • Internal bookkeeping and accounting purposes
  • Financial reporting and analysis
  • Tax preparation

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share QuickBooks data with any third parties. Data is accessed only by authorized personnel for legitimate business purposes.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect data accessed through this Application, including secure credential storage and encrypted connections.

Data Retention

Data accessed from QuickBooks Online is used in real-time and is not stored separately unless required for specific business purposes such as reporting or backup.
